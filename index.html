For Microphone Permission:

1. Click the lock icon next to the URL at the top of your browser.

2. In the dropdown menu, locate Microphone.

3. Choose Allow to enable microphone access for this site.

For Pop-ups and Redirects:

1. Open your browser settings by clicking on the three dots or menu icon at the top right.

2. Search for "Pop-ups and redirects" in the settings search bar.

3. Select Allow for this site to enable pop-ups and redirects.
